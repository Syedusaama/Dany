  • Can I pay cash on delivery?

    Yes. You can choose to pay via cash on delivery. Please choose the option Cash on Delivery on the payment option and pay the rider in cash at the time of delivery.

  • Are there any hidden charges when I make a payment?

    No. There are no hidden charges. All the prices on the website are inclusive of taxes. You don’t have to worry about any other charges.

    Your order details may have not been captured by the system. The amount will be refunded to your account in the next 5-7 business days.
